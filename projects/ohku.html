õhku estonia

Brand Identity for an Estonian airline that is proudly steeped in culture.

the brief

BRAND // PRINT // DIGITAL

Create the brand identity for an Estonian airline that is connected to its roots.

the idea

Among the many values in Estonian culture, are connection and belonging with nature, with friends and family, and with oneself. Õhku Estonia is an airline with connection and belonging at its heart. Blue and navy represent the sky and the soil, reflected in the Estonian colours, and orange is used to bring connection and comfort to the branding.
