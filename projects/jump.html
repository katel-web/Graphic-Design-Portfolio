jump

The design of an extreme sports magazine that is authentic at its core and is inspired by athletes pushing their bodies, and their minds, to the limit.

the brief

EDITORIAL // PRINT

The founders of Stab magazine found their once forward thinking and edgy followers had become a consumerist demographic. They wanted to create a magazine with stories from real people for real people.

the idea

Through getting into the mindset of the subjects and readers of the magazine - thrill seekers, adrenaline rushers, boundary pushers - Jump was born. A gritty, adventurous ode to those who get a kick out of exploring our limits - and then moving them.
