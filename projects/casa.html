casa moderna

Concept design for a three day home & living expo in Mexico City.

the brief

BRAND // PRINT

Design the concept for a three day home & living expo in Mexico City to capture the essence of the event.

the idea

“Inspiration lives here” This concept recognises that you should feel inspired in your own home and within Casa Moderna. Casa Moderna serves to inspire and show its audience the many great elements hidden behind its doors. The hand crafted design was created to reveal the possibilities within the Expo and to tap into your dreams to reveal your dream home.
